Income Tax on ₹10 Lakh Salary in India (AY 2025-26) — Full Calculation

Assumptions used in this example

  • Gross salary: ₹10,00,000 per year (basic + allowances included).
  • Standard deduction: as per applicable rule (presently ₹50,000) — we’ll apply the current available amount for AY 2025–26 where relevant.
  • Investments under Section 80C: assumed ₹1,50,000 (max allowable).
  • Mediclaim (80D): assumed premium ₹10,000.
  • No other income (capital gains, rental income etc.)
