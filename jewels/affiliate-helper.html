T24K — Affiliate Signup Helper

This local tool helps prepare and accelerate affiliate signups. It does not create accounts for you — it opens sign-up pages and pre-fills data you can copy/paste.

Each button opens the provider's signup page in a new tab. Copy/paste the prepared data below into their forms.
