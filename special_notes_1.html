Special Notes
Note: 1

In Old Regime if Total tax is less than or equal to 12,500.00 INR then your Tax will be zero bcz you will get Rebate else you have to pay Total tax.

In New Regime if Total tax is less than or equal to 25,000.00 INR then your Tax will be zero bcz you will get Rebate else you have to pay Total tax.

Note: 2

If you have not got the Rebate then extra 4% of Total tax is collected from you, towards "Health & Education Cess + Surcharge".

Note: 3

As a Professional Tax (PT) INR 200 per month is deducted & anually it comes to 2400 INR. While putting the gross, deduct this amount as well.

Note: 4

LTA & Gratuity calculations are not shown here but we will deal them separately.

Note: 5

House Loan details are not mentioned here but we will deal them separately.
