Refund Policy for Over payment

If you mistakenly overpay via our Razorpay payment gateway, you are eligible for a
refund of the excess amount upon request.

However, Razorpay transaction charges (typically ~2.36% including GST for domestic payments,
and ~3.54% for international payments) will be deducted from the refund amount.
The percentage mentioned is nearby & not exact.

Example for domestic: If you paid ₹9000 instead of ₹900, you may request a refund of ₹8100 minus gateway fees. That is 9000 - 9000*2.36/100 = 8787.6 [since 9000*2.36/100 = 212.4] Now deducting product cost 8787.6 - 900 = 7887.6 will be your refund amount. Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
