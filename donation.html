Make a Difference with Your Donation

At T24K, we believe in creating a better world through collective action. Your donation helps us implement impactful initiatives that transform lives. From supporting education and healthcare to driving environmental sustainability, every contribution matters.

Why Donate to T24K?

By contributing to T24K, you are supporting projects that:

  • Empower Education: Fund scholarships and educational resources for underprivileged children.
  • Enhance Healthcare: Provide medical assistance to those in need and support public health campaigns.
  • Promote Sustainability: Back eco-friendly projects that combat climate change and promote green practices.
  • Drive Social Change: Assist communities in developing skills, creating employment, and ensuring equal opportunities.

How Your Donation Helps

Transparency and accountability are at the core of our operations. Here is how we utilize your generous contributions:

  • 80% of your donation goes directly to projects and programs.
  • 15% supports operational expenses to ensure efficiency.
  • 5% is reserved for administrative costs to maintain transparency and compliance.

Donation Options
