Old vs New Tax Regime 2025 — Which is Better?

Compare the traditional (old) tax regime that allows deductions (80C, HRA, etc.) vs the simplified (new) regime with lower slab rates but fewer deductions.

How to decide

  • Calculate tax payable under both regimes for your income & deductions.
  • If total eligible deductions are high (80C, HRA, home loan), old regime might be better.
  • If few deductions/allowances, new regime often yields lower tax.
