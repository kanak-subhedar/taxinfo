1. Getting Started

What is T24K?

T24K is an innovative online platform designed to help individuals and businesses optimize their tax obligations. With tools for financial analysis, tax-saving strategies, and real-time alerts, T24K empowers you to take control of your tax planning.

Creating an Account

  1. Visit our homepage at T24K.com.
  2. Click on the Sign Up button in the top-right corner.
  3. Fill in your details, including email, password, and any required personal information.
  4. Verify your email address through the confirmation link sent to your inbox.

Navigating the Dashboard

Once logged in, you’ll land on the Dashboard. Here you can:

  • Access your financial summary.
  • View pending tasks.
  • Generate reports and insights.

2. Using T24K Effectively

Uploading Your Financial Data

To upload your financial data:

  1. Navigate to the Upload Data section.
  2. Choose from options to upload:
    • Bank statements
    • Payroll details
    • Expense records
  3. Ensure your data is accurate and up-to-date for the best results.

Understanding Tax Optimization Insights

Explore:

  • Tax Deductions: Learn about potential deductions specific to your financial situation.
  • Credit Maximization: Explore tax credits you qualify for, such as education or renewable energy credits.
  • Projections: See how future changes in income or expenses might affect your taxes.

Generating Tax Reports

Steps to generate tax reports:

  1. Go to the Reports section.
  2. Select the tax year and report type.
  3. Customize your report with filters, e.g., deductions, credits, or income sources.
  4. Download the report as a PDF or Excel file.
