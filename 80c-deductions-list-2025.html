Section 80C Deductions List (2025)

Section 80C allows a maximum overall deduction of up to ₹1,50,000 (confirm current limits) for eligible investments and payments. Below is a practical list with notes.

Common 80C items

  • EPF — employee contribution to EPF.
  • PPF — long-term safe option with tax-free returns.
  • ELSS — equity-linked mutual funds with shortest lock-in (3 years).
  • Life Insurance Premiums — individual policies for self/spouse/children.
  • Tuition fees — for up to two children (eligibility & limits apply).
  • Home loan principal repayment — principal portion repays under 80C.
  • Sukanya Samriddhi Account — for girl child, benefits and lock-in apply.

How to choose

Pick a mix of tax efficiency, liquidity needs and risk tolerance. ELSS offers growth but market risk; PPF gives stability; home loan principal helps homeowners.

Always keep proof of investments for tax filing/audit. Consult a CFP for portfolio alignment.
