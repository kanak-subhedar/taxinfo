How to File Income Tax Return (ITR) in India — 2025 Guide

Step-by-step guide for salaried taxpayers to prepare and file ITR electronically. Save screenshots and keep documents ready.

Checklist before filing

  • Form 16 from employer.
  • Bank interest certificates, FD TDS statements.
  • Investment proofs for 80C/80D, home loan statements.
  • Details of other income (rental, capital gains).

Filing steps (e-filing portal)

  1. Login to Income Tax e-Filing portal (with PAN & password).
  2. Choose ITR form (ITR-1 for most salaried individuals) and FY/AY.
  3. Pre-fill details or upload JSON generated by offline utility.
  4. Verify tax computation, declare deductions & income.
  5. Pay self-assessment tax (if any), submit and e-verify via Aadhaar OTP/NetBanking/DSC.

Common errors to avoid

  • Mismatched income values vs Form 16
  • Missing TDS / TCS credit reconciliation
  • Forgetting to e-verify within 120 days
If you prefer, consult a CA for first filing or complex returns with multiple incomes.
