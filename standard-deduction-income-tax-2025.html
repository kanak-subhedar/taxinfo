Standard Deduction in Income Tax (2025)

The standard deduction reduces taxable salary automatically. It applies to salaried individuals and pensioners — confirm the allowed amount for the year (previously ₹50,000 in many years).

Who is eligible?

Salaried employees under salary income and pensioners receiving pension from former employer’s pension schemes typically can claim the standard deduction.

How it works

Standard deduction is subtracted directly from gross salary to arrive at taxable salary, simplifying computations (no need to submit proofs unlike many other allowances).

Standard deduction amounts can be revised by the government in the budget — confirm the applicable value for AY 2025–26.
